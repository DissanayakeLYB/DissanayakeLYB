About Me


I am an undergraduate of Materials Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa, With the passion on this field I was focusing to broaden my knowledge-base by taking on a diverse curriculum covering core areas such as Metallurgy, Quantum Mechanics, and Smart Materials.

Beyond the classroom, I have honed my skills in Programming, Machine Learning, Computational Modeling, and also in fields such as Lean and Lean Six Sigma, allowing me to approach materials-related challenges from a multidisciplinary perspective.

With the idea of applying my enhanced problem solving skills, and knowledge on fields of expertise, I'm eager to work on projects with like-minded people and organizations to make an impact on the field of Materials Science and Engineering.
